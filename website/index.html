About Schedule.Rehab

Schedule.Rehab provides easy access to publicly available drug and alcohol support group meetings. Our mission is to help individuals in recovery find the support they need, when they need it.

All meetings listed are publicly accessible support groups. We aggregate information from various sources to provide a comprehensive calendar of available meetings.
